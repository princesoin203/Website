Most influential Rappers

Below is a photo gallary displaying my prefrence of the most influential rappers within modern rap culture. Please understand that this is my personal opinoun and their are many more rappers who deserve to be on this list. However this is condonsed down to my top 10 rappers who have changed rap culture for the better.

Gallary

This is Kendrick Lamar. One of the most influential rappers of this generation.
Kanye West much like Kendrick is a very influential figure within modern day rap. His impact on the fasion industry should also not be overlooked.
J cole's lyricism within his music is unmatched by any artist of this generation.
Eminem's rapping style and freestyling ability has been unmatched from his prime years of the early 2000's to this very year.
The reason lil uzi vert is on this list is because unlike other many other rappers uzi has defined one of the most unique styles seen to this day.
Polo g has continued to evolve since his hit song "Pop Out" came out in 2019.
Roddy Rich is unmatched.
Juice Wrld in his short time here on earth proved that he was well above the level of most rappers.
Sadly xxxTentation's time here on earth was also cut short. It would have been amazing to see what other music he could have released.
Although I am not a fan of Drake his influence on the culture of modern day rap cannot be overlooked. He is one of the largest artists on this planet.
Playboi Carti, similaraly to Uzi has a very unique style of rap which differentiates him from other rappers in the industry.
Tjay is a young talent who has proved to be talanted and skilled. It will be intresting to see where his carrer goes.
Travis Scott is has been influential in every aspect of his music carrer.
